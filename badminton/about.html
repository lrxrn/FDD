Who We Are

We are a passionate community of students who love badminton and aim to create a fun, inclusive, and competitive environment.

🎯 Our Mission

To promote fitness, teamwork, and a competitive spirit among students through the sport of badminton.

🎁 What We Offer

Weekly trainings, exciting tournaments, expert coaching, and a welcoming environment for all skill levels.

🤝 Why Join Us?

Whether you're a beginner or a pro, you'll find a place to grow, compete, and make new friends at APUFit Badminton Club.
