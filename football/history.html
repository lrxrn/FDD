Foundation

The club was founded in 2021 by a group of passionate local footballers who dreamed of making a mark in regional football. With humble beginnings, our dedication and community support helped us rise quickly through the amateur leagues.

Golden Era

Between 2022 and 2023, our club enjoyed its golden era, winning multiple regional championships and earning a reputation for producing top-tier talent. This era saw the rise of many players who later went on to play for national-level teams.

2024: Our club defeated the reigning champions in a thrilling 3-2 final to claim the regional cup.

Modern Times

Today, the club continues to thrive with an active youth academy, strong community programs, and consistent top-tier league participation. We aim to blend tradition with modern training techniques to stay competitive and continue making history.

2025: Club upgraded to a modern training facility with smart technology integration and fitness tracking.
