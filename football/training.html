Dribbling
Mastering dribbling helps you maintain control while moving and creates space from defenders.
Passing
Effective passing builds team play and advances the ball accurately to teammates.
Shooting
Learning to shoot accurately increases your scoring chances and impacts the match outcome.
Defending
Good defending requires positioning, anticipation, and tackling to stop opponents effectively.
Heading
Heading is key in both attack and defense, especially during set-pieces like corners.
Goalkeeping
Goalkeepers require agility, awareness, and courage to stop shots and command their area.
