Our Training Programs

Beginner Program

Perfect for those new to the sport. Focus on fundamental skills, rules of the game, and basic team tactics.

  • Dribbling fundamentals
  • Basic shooting techniques
  • Understanding court positions
  • Defensive basics

Intermediate Program

For players with basic knowledge seeking to improve their skills and game understanding.

  • Advanced dribbling techniques
  • Shooting under pressure
  • Team strategy development
  • Zone defense concepts

Advanced Program

For experienced players looking to compete at higher levels and refine their skills.

  • Advanced strategies and tactics
  • Game situation training
  • Leadership and team dynamics
  • Competition preparation
