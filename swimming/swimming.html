Founded in 2020, our club has quickly grown into a hub for swimming enthusiasts. We offer a range of programs, from beginner lessons to advanced training sessions, all led by our experienced coaching staff. Our mission is to foster a love for swimming while promoting health, fitness, and teamwork.

At APUFit Swimming Club, we offer:

  • Professional coaching for beginners, intermediates, and advanced swimmers
  • Regular training sessions to improve technique and endurance
  • Competitive opportunities for those looking to take their skills to the next level
  • Fun social events to build camaraderie and friendships
  • Access to top-notch facilities for training and competitions
  • Community outreach programs to promote swimming in the local area
  • Wellness and fitness programs to enhance overall health

We welcome players of all skill levels, from beginners looking to learn the basics to experienced athletes aiming to compete at higher levels.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To provide a safe and inclusive environment for swimmers of all levels to develop their skills, build confidence, and foster a lifelong love for the sport.

Vision

To be recognized as the leading swimming club in Malaysia, known for our commitment to excellence, community engagement, and athlete development.
