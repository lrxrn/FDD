Our Training Programs

Beginner Program

Start your swimming journey with safety and technique-focused lessons.

  • Water safety basics
  • Floating and breathing techniques
  • Basic strokes (freestyle, backstroke)
  • Pool rules & etiquette

Intermediate Program

Build your technique and endurance with structured drills and stroke correction.

  • Freestyle refinement
  • Breaststroke and backstroke
  • Flip turns & underwater kicks
  • Endurance training

Advanced Program

Train for competitive events with high-intensity workouts and expert coaching.

  • Race technique & starts
  • Individual medley training
  • Sprint & endurance sets
  • Meet preparation
